Global Warming

Global warming refers to the increase in the average temperature of the earth's surface during the last century. One of the reasons why global warming is dangerous is because it disturbs the overall ecology of the planet. This results in floods, famine, cyclones and other issues.



some of the causes of global warming :
  • Variations in the Sun's Intensity
  • Industrial Activity
  • Agricultural Activity
  • Deforestation
  • Earth's Own Feedback Loop
